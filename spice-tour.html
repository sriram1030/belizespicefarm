SPICE TOUR

Experience the Magic of Spices

  • Currently the Most popular and most requested tour
  • Duration - about an hour
  • Guide - Yes
  • Transportation - Tractor pulled trailer, golf cart or Tricycle cart, or by walking.
  • Areas to visit - All the Spice Blocks which include All spice, Nutmeg, Cinnamon, Vanilla and Black pepper. Visitors get to touch, smell and taste some of the spices directly from the plant.
