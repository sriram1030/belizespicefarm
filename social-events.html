SOCIAL EVENTS

Surrounded by Ornamental gardens, BSF&BG is an incredible setting for any Social event. The Orchid garden, Lotus canal, and serene landscape make a magical background where any photographer's imagination will run wild. We are away from big cities but have direct access to the highway. Our Cinnamon Hall with hand crafted Teak Ceiling, Teak and Rosewood floor, hand carved screwpine lights, along with a spacious deck is great for birthdays, graduations, and wedding parties.

The mystique and beauty of our facility start with its location – deep in the middle of Maya community with Maya villages and farms as our only neighbors. Not having the glaring lights, constant traffic, and the pollution that come with it make us unique and preferred by those who like a stress free, serene, natural setting for their social event.

Please contact our Social event planner for your next social event. We will make it a lasting experience with pleasant memories.

For Booking a social event please call 501 670 4906, WhatsApp number +501 670 6004 or +501 670 4906. You may also contact us by email.
